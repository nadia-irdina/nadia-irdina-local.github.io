Data Story

The Walking Dead

The Walking Dead is an American TV show that ran for 11 seasons since 2010 and ended with its last episode in 2022. It is set in a post-apocalyptic world and follows a group of survivors.

The group expands, travels and goes through many moral and physical challenges. Over time, members of this “family” get killed off, resulting in a high emotional reaction among its audience.

Audience Reception

This analysis aims to understand the implications of having a loyal fanbase, having a dramatic and gory genre, and having a long run time. It is expected for the show to undergo ups and downs.

Main Question:

What are some factors that may affect the longevity (and popularity) of the TV show: “The Walking Dead”?

Data Set

The data I am working with is taken from Kaggle, created by Bill Cruise and updated 9 months ago.

It has 2 csv files: episodes and IMDB
